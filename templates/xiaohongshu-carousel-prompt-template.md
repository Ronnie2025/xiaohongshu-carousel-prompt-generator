## 4. Publishing Check

- The cover says one concrete result.
- Each page only carries one job.
- The image prompt avoids QR codes, contact info, and unverifiable claims.
- The closing page does not promise traffic, followers, sales, or income.
- If selling a digital pack, the delivery file and after-sale boundary are stated clearly.
